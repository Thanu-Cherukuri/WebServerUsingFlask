002 : Hacker News Scraper

OVERVIEW:

This Python-based project presents a specialized Hacker News scraper designed to fetch and customize data from the Hacker News website. It effectively reads a list of URLs from a text file, scrapes specified pages for news articles, and processes the data to filter and sort the articles by the number of upvotes. The final output is a refined list of articles, each with more than 99 upvotes, thus ensuring a focus on popular and relevant news.

DEVELOPMENT PROCESS:

The development of this scraper began with a clear goal: to filter and sort the vast amount of content on Hacker News to find the most relevant and popular articles. The process involved designing an algorithm capable of reading URLs, scraping web pages, and processing the extracted data. A key focus was on developing a robust system that could handle the dynamic nature of web content while maintaining efficiency and accuracy.

UTILIZED TECHNOLOGIES:

Python 3.x:Chosen for its powerful libraries and ease of use in handling web scraping tasks.
BeautifulSoup: Utilized for its efficiency in parsing HTML and extracting necessary data.
Requests Library: Employed to fetch the content from the Hacker News website..

FEATURES:

Efficiently scrapes multiple pages of Hacker News.
Filters and includes only articles with more than 99 upvotes.
Sorts articles based on their upvote count.
Utilizes BeautifulSoup for streamlined HTML parsing.

INSIGHTS:

Through this project, I gained a deeper understanding of web scraping, data processing, and the challenges associated with extracting information from dynamic websites. The project highlighted the importance of writing efficient and adaptable code to handle various formats and structures of web content. Implementing the BeautifulSoup library for HTML parsing provided insights into the power of Python's ecosystem for data manipulation. The successful execution of this scraper reinforced my knowledge in web scraping and data filtering techniques, as well as the importance of presenting information in an accessible and relevant format.
